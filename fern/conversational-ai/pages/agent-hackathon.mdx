## How to Participate

<Steps>
  ### Preregister your intention to participate 
    Fill in the [registration form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe6QAV153jAfwREIJJzLyT8qc2XX6JfOMrG-jqv1T6quJUpew/viewform?usp=header)

  ### Build Your Agent
    You have 2 hours during the live event to create your voice agent using the ElevenLabs platform.

  ### Create Demo Video
    After the hackathon ends, you have 24 hours to create and submit a video demonstration of your agent. The submission link will be posted here later.

  ### Submit Your Entry
    Submit your video demo before the deadline to qualify for judging.

  ### Await Results
    Winners will be announced 1 week after the submission deadline.
</Steps>

<Note>
All participants will qualify for both the main ElevenLabs prize **and** can also submit to one partner track for additional prizes.
</Note>

<Warning>
Any updates or modifications to your agent after the 2-hour time limit will result in the submission no longer qualifying.
</Warning>
